# Investigating Equations of Springs in Series in Parallel

## Aim

To confirm that $k_{TOT}=k_1+k_2$ (Parallel) and that ${1\over k_{TOT}}={1\over k_1}+{1\over k_2}$ (Series)

## Apparatus

### In series

![Series and parallel springs - Wikipedia](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1f/SpringsInSeries.svg/300px-SpringsInSeries.svg.png)

### In parallel

![Series and parallel springs - Wikipedia](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/17/SpringsInParallel.svg/1200px-SpringsInParallel.svg.png)

## Method

1. For each spring:
   1. Measure natural length
   2. Attach a fixed mass $m$
   3. Let system come to rest
   4. Measure extension
   5. Use $ mg=k_nx to calculate  $k_n$
