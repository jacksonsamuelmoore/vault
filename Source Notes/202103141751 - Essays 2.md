# Comparative advantage essay
- Comparative advantage is the theory where because a country can produce more efficiently they should specialize and then trade rather than spending resources on other outputs.
- For: 
	- Specialization of workers leads to even more efficiency
	- No reason no to trade
- Against:
	- Developing industries
	- Sometimes forces trade when the countries are disadvantaged
	- Keeps countries that can only compete in agriculture or other primary goods by using cheaper labor costs stuck without development
