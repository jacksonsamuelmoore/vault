## 5.1 Globalisation

### What is globalisation?

Globalisation is the process of individual economies becoming more interdependent and forming tighter international webs

### What are the key causes of globalisation?

- Advancements in transport (containterisation)
- Economies of scale
- Reduction in protectionism
