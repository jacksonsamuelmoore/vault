# What are the effects of the removal of a tariff

### Starter

Draw a diagram to show the impact of a free trade deal between UK and Kenya/Ghana

![[What are the effects of the removal of a tariff/Untitled.png]]

Prices fall and consumer surplus rises. There is larger excess demand, leading to more imports. Government revenue falls and producer revenue falls. Simultaneously, producer surplus falls. However there is a general increase in welfare surplus, i.e. a net welfare gain.
