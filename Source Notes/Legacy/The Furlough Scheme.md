# The Furlough Scheme

Class: Economics
Created: Nov 28, 2020 12:00 PM
Type: Notes
Unit(s): Combined

### What is it:

- Known as the Job Retention Scheme
- 1.0: From April, 80% of wages up to £2500/month
- Only applicable to shutdown businesses
- 2.0: From October 2/3 up to £2100/month

### The logic:

The pandemic is a one-off economic shutdown, the furlough scheme is there to hold out the economy till recovery from the pandemic

### Advantages

- Workers keep jobs
- Continue to earn income
- Demand maintained
- No need for job seekers allowance or universal credit
- Maintain employment security
- Time to prep for alternatives
