### X-Efficiency

> X-inefficiency is a specific type of productive inefficiency. It occurs when a firm is not producing at it's lowest cost given a specific output

Reasons:

- Poor management (staff will become inefficient)
- Stakeholders end up with extra benefits
- Environmental pressure
