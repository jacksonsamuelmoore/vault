### Starter

Trade Bloc: An agreement between countries intended to reduce or remove trade barriers within member countries

Trade blocs tend to be made of countries near eachother

> Double the distance half the trade

### The EU single market

Features of the EU single market:

- Free movement of:
    - Goods
    - Services
    - Capitol
    - People
- Standardisation: Avoid barriers to entry and protectionism while maintaining standards
- Conformity mark (CE): See above
- Governance: Hold countries accountable to these features
- Mutual recognition of qualification
- Passporting: Banks can operate anywhere

Advantages of the EU:

- Protectionism-free trade
- Free movement of labour
- Level playing field
- Remittance flows

Disadvantages:

- Cost for access
- Free movement of labour
- EU laws could constrain businesses
- Trade diversion effects

### Customs unions

Features:

- Removes internal tariffs
- Common external tariffs
- Cannot negotiate trade deals
