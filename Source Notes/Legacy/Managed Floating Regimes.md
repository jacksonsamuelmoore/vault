### Weighing up

Advantages:

- Less intense propping up needed
- Allows for room to improve economy, more monetary autonomy
- More certainty, more investment

Disadvantages:

- More clear speculation target
- No self correction
- Still requires prop-up

### Why might countries devalue currency in managed floating?

Winners:

- Export businesses - relatively cheaper exports ⇒ More exports
- Government - more tariff income, improves balance of trade
- Economy - Reduce the risk of deflation

Loosers:

- Consumers - less choice, higher prices
- Economy - Inflation
