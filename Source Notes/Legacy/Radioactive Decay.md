### Band A ($\beta^-$)

Above the green, the neutron to proton ration is too high. To fix this a neutron decays into a proton: $n\rarr p^++e^-+\bar{v}_e$ (adding a $\beta^-$ to conserve charge and $\bar{v}_e$ or antineutrino to conserve lepton number)

Conservation of charge: $0\rarr 1-1+0=0$

Conservation of lepton number: $0\rarr 0+1-1=0$

Conservation of baryon number: $1\rarr 1+0+0=1$

General equation: $X_z^a\rarr Y_{z+1}^{a}+\beta^-$

### Band B ($\beta^+$)

Below the green, the neutron to proton ratio is to low. To fix this, a proton decays into a neutron: $p^+\rarr n +e^++v_e$ (add a $\beta^+$ to conserve charge and $v_e$ or neutrino to conserve lepton number)

Conservation of charge: $1\rarr0+1+0=0$

Conservation of lepton number: $0\rarr0+1-1=0$

Conservation of baryon number: $1\rarr 1+0+0=1$

General equation: $X_z^a\rarr Y_{z-1}^{a}+\beta^+$

### Others

Alpha: $X_z^a\rarr Y_{z-2}^{a-4}+\alpha$

Gamma: $X_z^a\rarr X_{z}^{a}+\gamma$

### Quark conversions

$\beta^+\rarr p^+(u,u,d)\rArr n(u,d,d)$ ← Quark flavour changed (up → down)

$\beta^-\rarr n(u,d,d)\rArr p^+(u,u,d)$ ← Quark flavour changed (down → up)

These changes are due to the weak force
