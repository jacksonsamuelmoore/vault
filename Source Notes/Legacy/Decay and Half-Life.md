### Starter

Radioactive decay is random and spontaneous - we can not predict when and which particles will display

- Activity:  $A=\lambda N$
- Decay constant: ($\lambda$) is the probability of decay of an individual nucleus, units of $s^{-1}$
- $N_{(t)}=N_0e^{-\lambda t}$
- Half life: $t_{1\over 2}$, The average time is takes for half of a substance to decay
    - $T_{1\over 2}={ln(2)\over \lambda}$
