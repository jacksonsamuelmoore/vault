### Strength

Gravitational field strength is force per unit mass â‡’ Electric field strength is force per unit charge

$E={F  \over |Q|}$ (**Positive charge**)

$W=F*d\\Eq=F\\W=qEd\\W/q=V\\W=Vq=qEd\implies V=Ed\implies E=V/d$  Where $d$ is the distance between the plates

Also:

$F={VQ \over d}$ (from $E=F/Q$  & $E=V/d$ )
