### In parallel

$Q_{TOT}=Q_1+Q_2\\C_{TOT}V_{TOT}=C_1V_1+C_2V_2\\C_{TOT}=C_1+C_2$

These are the opposite formula to resistors - as capacitors and resistors have opposite functions

### In series

$V_{TOT}=V_1+V_2\\Q_1=Q_2\\Q_{TOT}/C_{TOT}=Q_1/C_1+Q_2/C_2\\1/C_{TOT}=1/C_1+1/C_2$
