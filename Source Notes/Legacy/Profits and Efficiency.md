## Productive efficiency

Productive efficiency is operating at the lowest point of the cost curve. This is at  the intersection MC=AC, or in the long run is at the MES

![[Profits and Efficiency/Untitled 1.png]]

Suggested policies

- Use less resources
- Sack workers
- Renegotiate costs

Pareto efficient

- Both points are productively efficient
- They are pareto efficient as one cannot be added to without removing some from the other
