### Evaluate the benefits of the trans-pacific partnership

The trans-pacific partner ship is a large-scale trade agreement between several countries designed to be a link between a group of countries in Australasia and a group of countries in the Americas. The main effects of the agreement were the removal or cuts for 18,000 tariffs.

One of the main benefits is the increase in jobs for middle class workers. the USA cited exports contributing to over 11 million jobs total, the TPP creating roughly 1.5 million of those in the time since ratification. This increase in jobs would clearly create a welfare increase for the middle class, a certainly good thing. However, this may not be the only effect on jobs. Joining the TPP creates opportunity for the production of certain goods to be moved overseas. Wages in some of the of joining countries are lower than others, and this would create incentive to import certain goods that may have not been imported before, as they would be cheaper. This could create job losses for some sectors, particularly creating structural unemployment.
