### How could speculation cause problems for fixed exchange rates?

- Every downward pressure on the currency requires an upward 'prop up'
- Investors see this and know that it is being propped up and that it is overvalued
- This leads to them attacking the currency by selling much of their currency
- This leads to a giant crash in the market

### The policy trilemma

![[Costs and Benefits of Exchange Regimes/20160827_EBC992.jpg]]

- Fixed exchange rate + capital mobility = No monetary autonomy
- Fixed exchange rate + monetary autonomy = Can't allows capital mobility
- Capital mobility + monetary autonomy = No fixed exchange rate
