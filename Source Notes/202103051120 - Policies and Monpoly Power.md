# How should the government handle monopolies
Continued from [[202103040857 - Monopoly Power]]

## Policies examples
### Price capping:
Advantages:
- Increases allocative efficiency
- Better economies of scale

Disadvantages:
- May be wrong price
- May cost jobs
- Reduce investment
### Tax on profits
Advantages:
- Redistribute excess profits in one area for the greater social good

Disadvantages:
- Might increase prices
- Mat cost jobs
- Reduce investment

### Nationalization:
Advantages:
- Control over motives and price setting
- More efficient with lower interest rates

Disadvantages:
- Often less quality than private as there's not much motive to improve
- Government losses due to costs of subsidies 

Applicable: Utilities (like water)
